Volume and Surface Area of an Icosahedron

Calculate for Volume and Surf Area:



This webpage is for calculating the Volume and Surface Area of an Icosahedron. An Icosahedron is a shape that has 20 faces. The formula for the Volume is V = 5(3+sqrt5)/12*a3, and the formula for Surface Area is A = 5*sqrt3*a2.
